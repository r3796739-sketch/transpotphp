Personnel Placement — Qualified. Vetted. Ready.

Special One Cars GmbH provides professional personnel placement services for the transport and logistics sector. We recruit, screen, and deploy qualified drivers, warehouse staff, and logistics coordinators to meet your operational needs — whether you require temporary cover, seasonal support, or permanent hires.

Our recruitment process includes background checks, license verification, and skills assessment to ensure every candidate meets industry standards. We work with businesses of all sizes, from startups to established logistics firms, offering flexible staffing solutions tailored to your requirements.

  • Comprehensive background checks and license verification for all candidates.

  • Flexible staffing: temporary, contract, and permanent placement options.

  • Specialized roles: professional drivers, warehouse staff, logistics coordinators.

  • Fast deployment with ongoing support and performance monitoring.

Professional Drivers

Licensed drivers with clean records, professional training, and experience in passenger or freight transport.

Logistics Staff

Warehouse operators, coordinators, and support staff trained in inventory management and safety protocols.

How quickly can you provide staff?

For urgent requirements, we can deploy pre-screened candidates within 24-48 hours. Permanent placements typically take 1-2 weeks depending on role complexity.

What screening do candidates undergo?

All candidates undergo background checks, license verification, reference checks, and skills assessments. Drivers also complete safety training and vehicle operation tests.

Do you offer replacement guarantees?

Yes. For permanent placements, we offer a 90-day replacement guarantee. If a candidate doesn't work out, we'll provide a replacement at no additional charge.

Can you handle seasonal or project-based staffing?

Absolutely. We specialize in flexible staffing solutions including seasonal peaks, project-based work, and temporary coverage for absences or leave.

What are your fees?

Fees vary by role type and placement duration. Contact us for a detailed quote based on your specific requirements. We offer competitive rates and volume discounts for ongoing partnerships.
