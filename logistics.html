Logistics Solutions — Efficient. Reliable. Scalable.

Special One Cars GmbH delivers end-to-end logistics solutions designed to streamline your supply chain operations. From freight transport and warehousing to last-mile delivery and inventory management, we provide comprehensive services that ensure your goods move efficiently from origin to destination — on time, every time.

Our logistics network covers Berlin and extends across Germany and Europe. We leverage modern tracking technology, optimized routing, and experienced logistics coordinators to minimize costs and maximize delivery performance. Whether you're a small business or a large enterprise, we tailor our solutions to match your operational needs.

  • Full-service freight transport: local, regional, and international shipping.

  • Warehousing and inventory management with real-time tracking systems.

  • Last-mile delivery solutions for e-commerce and retail businesses.

  • Custom logistics planning with dedicated account management and reporting.

Freight Transport

Reliable freight services for palletized goods, parcels, and specialized cargo with flexible scheduling and competitive rates.

Warehousing & Distribution

Secure storage facilities with inventory management, order fulfillment, and distribution services for seamless operations.

What types of goods can you transport?

We handle a wide range of cargo including palletized freight, parcels, documents, and specialized items. For hazardous materials or oversized cargo, please contact us for specific requirements and certifications.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes. We provide international freight services across Europe with customs clearance support, documentation assistance, and cross-border logistics coordination.

Can I track my shipment in real-time?

Absolutely. All shipments include real-time tracking with GPS monitoring, delivery notifications, and online access to shipment status through our customer portal.

What are your warehousing capabilities?

We offer secure, climate-controlled warehousing with inventory management systems, order picking and packing, and distribution services. Storage solutions are available for short-term and long-term needs.

How do you handle delivery delays or issues?

We proactively monitor all shipments and communicate any potential delays immediately. Our customer service team works to resolve issues quickly, and we maintain insurance coverage for cargo protection.
